Algebraic Geometry II (V4A2), Summer semester 2016

Dozent: Prof. Daniel Huybrechts

Assistent: Andrey Soldatenkov

This is a continuation of Algebraic Geometry I last term. We study further properties of schemes and morphisms. Central notions like flatness and smoothness will be investigated. We will also see all the techniques applied to geometrically concrete situations.

Prerequisits: Sheaves, cohomology of sheaves, schemes, Proj and Spec, coherent sheaves, properties of morphisms (proper, separated, projective).

Lectures

Monday 16.00 - 18.00, Großer Hörsaal, Wegelerstr. 10 and Thursday 14.00 - 16.00, Großer Hörsaal, Wegelerstr. 10

  • Literature:
    • R. Hartshorne: Algebraic Geometry GTM 52. Springer. (This is still the most commonly used source.)
    • D. Mumford: The Red book of varieties and schemes. Springer LN 1358.
    • R. Vakil: Foundations of algebraic geometry. Online lectures
    • J. de Jong: The Stacks project
    • D. Eisenbud and J. Harris, The Geometry of Schemes, GTM 197, Springer.
    • U. Goertz, T. Wedhorn: Algebraic Geometry I. Vieweg.
    • Q. Liu: Algebraic Geometry and Arithmetic Curves. Oxford GTM
